Passing Ladder

  • Right foot Up
  • Left foot back
  • On toes, light feet
  • Move to ball, don't wait
  • Can be 1 touch
  • Can be 2 touch
  • Coach calls out if 1 or 2 touch
  • Urgent and focus
Why is this important?
  • Works on basic skill
  • Accuracy
  • Move to and away from ball
  • Works both feet
  • Works on being on toes, ready position

Weave to Shoot

Simple touch, wall pass, shot

  • Use pole as obstacle
  • Shuffle back an forth on toes behind pole
  • Recieve ball, turn, play wall
  • Go around pole, play back to teamate or coach
  • Repeat
Why is this important?
  • Keeps constant movement, on toes
  • Works turn, receive
  • Simple first touch

Partner Calls

  • Partner yells out call
  • 1: Go Right, Say Right and point Right. Player touches right, plays back (2 touch)
  • 2: Go Left, Say Left and point Left. Player touches left, plays back (2 touch)
  • Pressure: Play back(drop), say pressure (or man on), 1 touch
  • Turn: Say turn, Player uses a turn moves forward and shoots
  • Be urgent
Why is this important?
  • Works on player to player communication
  • Works on touch, turns, movement
