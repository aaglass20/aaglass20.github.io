Circle Relay

A competitive team drill combining sprinting and ball control, where players must sprint between points and demonstrate controlled dribbling in a relay format.

    • Two teams
    • Start at middle
    • Run one way, touch end
    • Sprint to the other end
    • Dribble ball to other circle and stop
    • Next group goes once ball rest
    Why is this important?
    • Fitness
    • Control
    • Relay
    • Head up dribbling
