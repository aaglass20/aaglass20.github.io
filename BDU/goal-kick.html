Build-Out Goal Kick

A tactical goal kick drill focusing on building play from the defensive third against active pressure. Players work on optimal positioning and spacing, creating diagonal passing lanes while facing opposition from central areas. The drill emphasizes proper spreading of defenders, strategic ball placement from the goalkeeper, and decision-making under pressure from central attackers trying to win possession. Perfect for developing organized build-out play patterns and understanding how to break the first line of pressure from goal kicks.
