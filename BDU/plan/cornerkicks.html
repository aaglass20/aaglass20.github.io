Offensive Corner

Option 1: Simple Crash (Kicker drops arm to start)

  • Back Door stays
  • 2 Trailers run
  • Front Door stays
  • Top of the box stays

Option 2: Two players go to corner

  • Small touch to outside player
  • Players separate
  • Option to pass, cross, or shoot

Option 3

  • Kicker calls short
  • Front door player shows and receives the ball
  • Passes back to kicker
  • Kicker then plays the ball

Defending Corner

  • At least 1 forward stays high as an available outlet
  • Left and Right Defenders guard the posts
  • Center Defender is free and calls out marks
  • Everyone else is free to move as needed
  • Center Defender is the "magnet" to the ball
  • Right and Left hold ground, but attack the ball if it’s theirs
  • Midfielders mark tightly and hunt the ball
  • Attitude of “the ball is ours”: clear quickly
  • Keeper calls for the ball; if they call it, they must secure it
