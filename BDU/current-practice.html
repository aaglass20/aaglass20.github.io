Passing/Shooting Drill

Quick combination play focusing on diagonal movement. Player A makes diagonal pass, Player B shows for the ball, redirects it across to Player C, who then takes the shot. Emphasizes quick passing, off-ball movement, and finishing.

Triple Threat Throw

Three attackers set up similar to corner formation: first player breaks near, second attacks center, third drifts wide. Utilizes long throw specialist to deliver ball with corner-kick power into dangerous areas.

Cross to Volley or 1 timer

Three attackers time their runs as winger prepares cross: first crashes near post, second attacks center, third drifts back post. Each ready for one-touch finish or volley. Timing and spacing critical for avoiding defenders.
