You lose your man, you run

4v4 or 3v3. You are man marking. Coach is the outlet or goalie. Players try to get open. If your player your defending gets open, you run and they play down until you get back to your player.

4v4 Man Marking Drill

Small field, 4 players per team, 1 goalie per team. Each player is responsible for marking one opponent. The objective is to maintain tight marking while allowing the goalie to play the ball out. This drill emphasizes the importance of communication, positioning, and understanding of marking principles.

Pressing 2 V 3

Red vs Yellow Team. Red team has 3 players, Yellow team has 2 players. The yellow plays it out. Red swarms two to the ball. The other red player man marks the other yellow player. The yellow team is trying to pass the ball in the small goal to win. The Red team is pressing towards the large goal

Transitional Shooting, defensive track down

Red team starts by shooting on goal. Red team player touches goal post. As soon as that happens, the yellow team takes off with ball and the Red player attempts to stop. As soon as play is dead, the yellow player touches goal poast and the Red team takes off with ball. Drill is continued.
