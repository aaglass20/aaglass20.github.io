  • Jog 1 lap

Long, Short, Short, Long pass

Simple warmup drill combining long, short, short, long passes to prepare for match situations.

Get Comfort with Physical Contact

~5-10 min warmup. Get comfortable with body contact before defensive drills.

Playing the Ball & Press Delay

Practice playing the ball under pressure and delaying the press.

  • Banana Run
    • Pressure - closing the distance
    • Patience - look for the mistake
    • Position - make them go right or left
  • Delay offensive attack at all cost
    • Worst thing you can do is dive in
    • Delay to push out of the goal
  • Staggered stance, knees bent, stay on your toes
  • 90% is decision making
    • Is it a block?
    • Is it a tackle?
    • Is it a clearance?
