Defensive Training Plan

About This Plan

This defensive training plan focuses on building solid defensive fundamentals through progressive daily sessions. Each day builds upon the previous, covering topics like positioning, 1v1 defending, team shape, pressing, and recovery runs. Complete each day's drills before moving to the next.

Defensive Attitude

"Tackle Hard. Every Time."

"When you tackle, you tackle hard."

There is not one thing soft or delicate or nice about tackling.

Commit fully. Don't just dangle a leg in and hope for the best. When you go in for a tackle, you go in with intent.
Follow through. If you commit for a tackle, see it through to completion. Half measures get you beat.
Win the ball. The goal is to dispossess your opponent and regain possession for your team.

50/50 Ball Mentality

"Make Every 50/50 a 100/0"

"Never lose a 50/50 ball again."

A 50/50 ball is only 50/50 if you let it be. Your desire determines the outcome.

Want it more. The player who wants the ball more will win it. Bring intensity and hunger to every contest.
Get there first. Anticipate, react quickly, and arrive before your opponent has time to settle.
Use your body. Get low, be strong, shield the ball. Make yourself impossible to move.
