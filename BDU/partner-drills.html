Progressive Partner Skills Series

Comprehensive seven-station partner training sequence progressing from basic passing accuracy through advanced technical skills. This circuit combines foundational elements like cone passing and technical passing with dynamic movements, positional awareness, 1v1 scenarios, ball control, and finishing. Each drill builds upon the previous, creating a complete technical development session that maximizes practice time with just two players.

1. Pass through cones

2. Technical Pass

3. Pass and Move

4. Straight - diagonal passing

5. Sprint/Tight/Attack 1v1's

6. Juggles

7. Pass and Shoot

Two Touch Box

A dynamic three-player possession drill centered around quick ball control and precise passing. One player positions inside a small box while two others station outside on opposite ends. The inside player receives passes alternately from the outside players, required to control and distribute the ball using exactly two touches. The first touch sets up the second, which must be a clean pass to the opposite outside player. This drill sharpens essential skills including directional first touch, passing accuracy, spatial awareness, and quick decision-making. Ideal for developing the fundamental rhythm of receive-and-release play common in match situations, while the confined space promotes efficiency in movement and ball control.
