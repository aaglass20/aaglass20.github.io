Offsides Example 1

Group on Left is onside<
  • Receiving player is behind midline when ball is struck
  • Once ball is struck Player can move beyond the midline
  • Receiving player is behind the last defender, but before the midline which keeps them onside
Group on Right is offside<
  • Receiving player is over midline past the last player when ball is struck
  • They are offside

Offsides Example 2

Group on Left is onside<
  • The new offsides line is the last defender
  • The Receiving player is even with last defender
  • When the ball is stuck, receiving player can take off and they are not offside
Group on Right is offside<
  • The new offsides line is the last defender
  • The Receiving player past the line/player
  • When the ball is stuck, receiving player is offside/li>
