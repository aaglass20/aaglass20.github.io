BDU Drills - Offense - Relays

1 V 1 Dribble Relayl

  • Two lines
  • Dribble and around each pair of cones
  • Go around pole, race back
  • Once line crossed, next goes
  • Start as drill before racint
Variations
  • Put Agility Pole at finsh
  • Put Rings on Poles
  • Player must take one ring from one pole and transfer to the other

Circle Relay

  • Two teams
  • Start at middle
  • Run one way, touch end
  • Sprint to the other end
  • Dribble ball to other circle and stop
  • Next group goes once ball rest
Why is this important?
  • Fitness
  • Control
  • Relay
  • Head up dribbling

Feint, dribble, controlled shot

  • Dribble to wall, feint
  • next player feint opposite side
  • controlled dribble
  • nice low level shot with side of foot
  • get ball, go to outside, speed dribble back
  • rejoin line
  • Could relay race
Why is this important?
  • Learning to feint
  • Controlled dribble
  • controlled side of the foot shot
  • Speed on way back, fitness

Pass, Move, Communicate

    Rules

    • Start with a Pass
    • Then a move, player without ball
    • Then repeat
    • Goal is to get the ball in the circles
    • Team is through when all players make it in the circle
    • Communication is big on who moves and where the pass is going
    • Start being turn based, then remove that restriction
