New 50 Touches

  • Long pass (Dominant foot)
  • Short pass (Dominant foot)
  • Turn
  • Short Pass (Non Dominant foot)
  • Rotate
Why is this important?
  • Keeping player on toes
  • If done right, constant movement
  • Coming to the ball opposed to waiting

Two lines warmup.

  • Basic movement
  • Leading 1 or 2 passes
  • Shoot with some distance
Why is this important?
  • Get loose
  • Leading pass
  • Work on strong shot

Dynamic warmup

  • P2 back to P1

  • As P2 gets passed, take off around P3
  • P3 passes to P1
  • p1 back to P2
  • P2 Shoots
  • P1 replaces P3
  • P3 replaces P2

Throw In Loop

  • Defender Throw ball in
  • Forward and Mid Line up next to thrower
  • Forward Loop run around Mid
  • Defender ties run and throws down the line
Why is this important?
  • Attempt to pick defender
  • Cause Movement

Throw In Cross

  • Defender Throw ball in
  • Forward and Mid Line up next to thrower
  • Forward and Mid Criss Cross
  • Defender looks for open run, times run
Why is this important?
  • Cause Movement to free player

Shooter Vs Defence

  • 5 balls lined up at 18 yard line
  • Striker on ones side, Defender on other
  • Defense is only allowed to shuffle and get body in front to stop strike
  • Sticker runs back and forth and only shoots when finding and opening
  • D is only trying to block
Why is this important?
  • Works on body placement for D
  • Urgency on closing in for D
  • Works on quick shot
