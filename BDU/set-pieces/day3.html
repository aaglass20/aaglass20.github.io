Day 3: Throw-ins

Training session 3 of 4

Drill 1: Long Throw Technique

10 min Individual/Pairs

Foundation drill to build core technique and confidence.

  • Focus on proper technique
  • Start slow, build speed gradually
  • Quality over quantity
  • Both feet/sides

Drill 2: Quick Throw Options

15 min Small Groups

Progress to more game-realistic situations with added complexity.

  • Increase tempo
  • Add decision making
  • Communication required
  • Competitive element

Drill 3: Throw-in Plays

15 min Groups

Apply skills under pressure with defensive or time constraints.

  • Game speed intensity
  • Pressure from opponents
  • Quick transitions
  • Reward success

Drill 4: Throw-in Situations

20 min Full Group

Game-based activity to apply all learned skills in match context.

  • Full game conditions
  • Encourage use of session skills
  • Praise good application
  • Fun competitive finish
