🧍‍♀️ Team Values and Identity

We played with passion, perseverance, and pride. While we knew we wouldn’t always be best friends off the field, we stood by this truth:

“We might not all be friends all the time, but we are friends on the soccer field.”

We succeeded as a team, failed as a team, and always showed up for one another.

Devils on three. One, two, three… DEVILS!

🎯 Our Personal Goals for This Season

  • Build deeper trust between players and coaches
  • Focus areas:
    • Refine individual technical skills and correct foundational habits
    • Raise the level of off-ball movement and awareness
    • Foster better on-field communication — become “field generals”
    • Learn to move the ball horizontally with purpose while maintaining our identity as an attacking team
    • Introduce advanced concepts:
      • Scanning the field
      • Making creative, off-ball runs
      • Seeing the game in new dimensions

⚔️ Style of Play & Team Structure

  • Our Identity: Attack-minded, high-pressure, clean physical play. Not bullies, but a force on the field.
  • Positional Versatility: Everyone will train in all positions, with natural specialization over time.
  • Playing Time:
    • Everyone gets at least half a game
    • Hard work in practice earns extra time
    • We have 5 on the bench — rotation matters
    • Still playing 9v9
    • Regular season = development
    • Tournaments = compete to win
