Two lines warmup.

  • Basic movement
  • Leading 1 or 2 passes
  • Shoot with some distance
Why is this important?
  • Get loose
  • Leading pass
  • Work on strong shot

Dynamic warmup

  • P1 pass to P2
  • P2 back to P1
  • As P1 makes pass to P3, P1 takes off around P2
  • As P2 gets passed, take off around P3
  • P3 passes to P1
  • p1 back to P2
  • P2 Shoots
  • P1 replaces P3
  • P3 replaces P2
Why is this important?

Cross Drill

  • From either Left or Right, cross ball
  • Offense should have leead, trailer, and Garbage runner
  • Lead goes inside
  • Trailer goes Middle delayed
  • Garbage lurks backside goal box
Why is this important?
  • Work on Cross Technique
  • Works on off ball positioning

Simple Kickoff - Drop and go

  • Forward drops to Mid
  • Outside Mids take off
  • Sriker also takes off
  • Center mid hits left or right corner
  • Variation: Mid or forward does same without drop
Why is this important?
  • Quick way to get down field
  • Drop back gives time for mids to get down field

Group 1 - Through Ball Drill

  • Work right or left side
  • Runner explodes after pass back
  • receiver of pass back can yell drop>
  • When playing through, say Name and through
  • When player is receiving drop pass, work on calling for drop
  • player starting with ball is the main communicator
Why is this important?
  • Communicate what type of ball your playing
  • Communicate to who you are playing to
  • This allows other teammates to keep width
  • Works on drop pass
  • drop passes relieve pressure
  • Communications is not just words, its gestures, motion
  • Works on finishing on the run
