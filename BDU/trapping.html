Trap Control

  • Maintain control on each line
  • Pass back after controlled
  • line 1, low control
  • line 2, mid high control
  • line 2, chest control
  • Right foot up the ladder
  • Left foot down the ladder
  • Shuffle body
  • don't raise foot, keep it low
Why is this important?
  • Works on keeping foot in trap position
  • Works on keeping arms down on higher balls
  • Proper trap technique
  • Shuffling body to ball instead of foot to ball
  • Works on playing the ball immediately after settling
  • Works on being on toes, ready position
