Day 1: Finishing Basics

Clinical finishing from inside the box

Drill 1: Striking Technique

10 minutes Individual Beginner

Stationary ball shooting. Focus on proper technique - plant foot, locked ankle, strike through the ball, follow through toward target.

  • Plant foot beside ball, pointed at target
  • Lock ankle, toe down for laces shot
  • Strike through center of ball
  • Follow through toward target
  • Accuracy first, power second

Drill 2: Side Foot Finishing

15 minutes With Server Beginner

Passes from the side, finish with inside of foot. The most reliable finishing technique - placement over power.

  • Open body to see goal and ball
  • Side foot for accuracy
  • Pick your corner before the ball arrives
  • Aim low - hard for keeper to save
  • Follow the shot in case of rebound

Drill 3: One-Touch Finishing

15 minutes With Server Intermediate

Ball played across goal, one-touch finish. Simulate real game situations where you don't have time to control.

  • Body shape ready before ball arrives
  • Attack the ball, don't wait for it
  • Redirect with pace already on ball
  • Keep it on target - make keeper work
  • Low and hard beats keepers

Drill 4: Finishing Under Pressure

20 minutes With Defender Advanced

Receive ball with defender behind. Quick turn or one-touch finish. Learn to finish with a body on you.

  • Know where defender is before ball arrives
  • Use body to shield
  • Quick shot before defender can block
  • Composure - don't rush, don't panic
  • If no shot, hold up and wait for support

Key Coaching Points

  • Composure: Stay calm in front of goal - breathe
  • Placement: Accuracy beats power every time
  • Low Shots: Low shots are hardest to save
  • Follow Up: Always follow your shot for rebounds
  • Confidence: Believe you'll score before you shoot
