Day 4: 1v1 Skills & Creativity

Beat defenders and create magic

Drill 1: Skill Moves Practice

15 minutes Individual Beginner

Practice fundamental skill moves: step-overs, scissors, body feints, Cruyff turns. Master them without a defender first.

  • Step-over: Foot goes around, not over ball
  • Scissors: Quick feet, sell the fake
  • Body feint: Drop shoulder, explode opposite way
  • Cruyff turn: Drag ball behind standing leg
  • Practice both feet!

Drill 2: 1v1 to Goal

15 minutes Pairs Intermediate

1v1 situations from different angles. Attacker must beat defender and finish. Use moves practiced in drill 1.

  • Attack at speed - slow dribbling is easy to defend
  • Watch defender's hips, not feet
  • Change of pace beats defenders
  • Once past, finish quickly before recovery

Drill 3: Take On Challenge

15 minutes Groups Intermediate

Dribble through cones, then face defender. Must attempt to beat them. Points for successful take-ons and goals.

  • Commit to the take-on
  • Use skills under pressure
  • If move fails, recover and try again
  • Confidence is key - believe you can beat them

Drill 4: Creative Finishing Game

20 minutes Small Sided Advanced

Small-sided game where skillful goals count double. Encourage creativity - chips, rabonas, flicks, dribbles into box.

  • Express yourself - try new things
  • Take on defenders when 1v1
  • Look for creative finishes
  • Have fun - creativity comes from enjoyment

Key Coaching Points

  • Confidence: Belief in your ability to beat defenders
  • Change of Pace: Speed up/slow down to unbalance defender
  • Close Control: Keep ball close when approaching defender
  • Commit: Once you start a move, commit to it fully
  • Creativity: Express yourself - there's no wrong way to score
