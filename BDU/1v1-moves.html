1 v 1 Moves

Knee Rocket

Ground-to-air skill move: Draw ball back with one foot, flick upward, then drive opposite knee forward while turning to launch the ball. Creates deceptive movement combining ground control and aerial lift.

Four 1 V 1 Skills to beat defenders

Four essential dribbling techniques designed to help players beat defenders in one-on-one situations, combining speed, deception, and ball control.

Inside Drag

A fundamental ball control technique using the inside of the foot to drag and redirect the ball, essential for quick directional changes and maintaining close possession.

Move to Shot

Quick footwork drill combining deceptive moves with explosive shooting. Perfect for creating space and finishing in tight situations.

Be Deceptive

A deceptive movement drill where players practice sudden stops followed by explosive accelerations, mimicking game situations to create separation from defenders. This drill enhances change of pace, balance control, and defensive manipulation.

Flick Up Tutorial

A skillful technique where players use their foot to lift the ball off the ground in one fluid motion. This move is useful for initiating juggling, transitioning into aerial skills, or creating unexpected plays to beat defenders.
