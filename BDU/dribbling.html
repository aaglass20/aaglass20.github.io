Dribble & Draw Defenders

An advanced dribbling technique that focuses on purposefully engaging defenders to create tactical advantages. This skill demonstrates how to use controlled dribbling to draw defenders out of position, creating spaces and passing lanes for teammates. By deliberately moving toward opponents, players learn to manipulate defensive positioning, force defensive commitments, and exploit the resulting gaps. This fundamental attacking concept teaches players how to use individual skill to generate team-wide offensive opportunities.
