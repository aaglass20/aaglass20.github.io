Passing Ladder - Group 1 - Aaron

  • Right foot Up
  • Left foot back
  • On toes, light feet
  • Move to ball, don't wait
  • Can be 1 touch
  • Can be 2 touch
  • Coach calls out if 1 or 2 touch
  • Urgent and focus
Why is this important?
  • Works on basic skill
  • Accuracy
  • Move to and away from ball
  • Works both feet
  • Works on being on toes, ready position

  • Quick Shot

  • Defender should start by just body pressure, don't steal
  • Extension of Drill is defender trying to steal, but prob not today

  • Focus on being quick
  • Can go left or right after receiving, feel where pressure is

    More of step then a lunge, I'll demo

Pass, Shoot, Defend - Whole Team
