  • Use Right, Left, inside, and outside foot

Defensive Technique as a team

  • Offence slow dribbles
  • Warm up with "Flip" drill
  • Defense channels with "running like a Defender"
  • Shift hips if Off changes direction
  • Coach yells attack, attack
  • Lunge across Offences body
  • Arm first, post on body
  • Step across body and tackle
  • We look for openings to steal
  • Try it without coach influence
Why is this important?
  • Stepping across body slows down offence if you miss your tackle attempt
  • We don't poke, we body up
  • Be confident

Fun drill if we have time, make it a competition

Shooter Vs Defense

  • 5 balls lined up at 18 yard line
  • Striker on ones side, Defender on other
  • Defense is only allowed to shuffle and get body in front to stop strike
  • Sticker runs back and forth and only shoots when finding and opening
  • D is only trying to block
Why is this important?
  • Works on body placement for D
  • Urgency on closing in for D
  • Works on quick shot
