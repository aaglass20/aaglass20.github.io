Practice Plan
    Agenda
  • 15 min: warmup (taking this slow)
    • Stretch (working on being organized, together)
      • 1, 2, 3, Touch Toe
      • Lunges
      • Hug knee to Chest
      • Cradle Leg and Ankle
      • Criss cross quad
      • Skips
      • Stand in place X 10 - Count
        • Scissors * 10
        • Front to Back Hop * 10
        • Side to Side * 10
        • Criss Cross * 10
        • Hops * 10
    • Run
      • Sprint * 2
      • Flip Running * 4

    • Flip run breakdown
      • Everyone gets 4 to 5 cones
      • Do each dribble drill up and down until we say stop
      • These skills cover the main movements
        • Dribble Right Foot inside and outside
        • Dribble Left Foot inside and outside
        • Dribble both feet
        • Sole Roll Right Foot only
        • Sole Roll Left Foot only
        • Change of direction end
          • Turns
          • Lunge, butt out protect ball
          • Back Heal
          • Shift wight
    • Do Flip Running
      • Slow dribble, focus is work on dribbling skill, what do you want to master
      • Yell Flip or switch to get defender to rotate
      • Defender focus is on technique and anticipating the attack, keeping eye on hips and ball

    • New 50 touches Breakdown
      • Why are we doing this?
        • Proper technique for passing and receiving...make every touch count
        • Communication is part of our game
        • Moving with a purpose to always better our position on and off the ball
        • Works on 5 skills in one drill
          • Long Pass
          • Short Pass
          • Turns
          • Communications
          • Movement with purpose
        • Expectations
          • Work towards near perfect, don't rush your passes
          • The goal is to get better during this time, not a checklist
          • With movement, we want urgency
          • We communicate to aid our teammates

    • Breaking down 50 touches
      • 5 yards two touch with partner
        • Work on proper passs and receive, 2 touch
        • Communicate - work on saying each others names on your pass
        • Make a mistake, take a breathe, bring it back to where you should be
        • Coach observes and gives feedback
      • Turns (prob just do one of these today)
      • Examples in 3 Turns to Master
        • Kill & Push
          • Everyone has a ball
          • Show Kill & Push, really break it down
          • Try it
        • Open Up
          • Everyone has a ball
          • Show Kill & Push, really break it down
          • Try it
        • No Touch
          • Everyone has a ball
          • Show Kill & Push, really break it down
          • Try it
      • Soccer Wind Sprints - relay
        • 3 balls acting as cones
        • To tap first one
        • Sprint
        • To tap Second one
        • Sprint
        • To tap Third one
        • Back pedal
        • To tap Second one
        • Back pedal past line
        • Next one takes off
      • Communication points
        • Turn
        • Through
        • Say Name
        • Call for ball
        • Practice Soccer talking

    • Now do 50 Touches

      • New 50 Touches Warmup

      • Long pass
      • Short pass
      • Turn

    We won't get there, but if we do....nice relay to end practice and get some more running in

    Circle Relay

    • Two teams
    • Start at middle
    • Run one way, touch end
    • Sprint to the other end
    • Dribble ball to other circle and stop
    • Next group goes once ball rest
    Why is this important?
    • Fitness
    • Control
    • Relay
    • Head up dribbling
