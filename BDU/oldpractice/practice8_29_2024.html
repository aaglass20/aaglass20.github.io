Heads up dribbling

  • Dribble with head up, coach changes color or hold up finger count
  • Team yells color while maintaining dribble

Quick Throw

  • Explain quick throw and let move away from waiting
  • Throw to teammate, teammate traps, lays off out of bounds
  • Thrower practices entering the field
  • Thrower sprints to ball to throw again
  • Receiver takes off to receives
  • Shoot on goal on last one, repeat on other side of field, new thrower

Working the sideline, dealing with pressure

  • This vid has several drills in it
  • Coach plays on side line, we work on taking an outside touch with the foot nearest to goal
  • Be natural with it to turn up or play out of bounds back to coach
  • Variation: Add defender that is in chase down mode
  • Work on using your body to shield, play up or out
  • Variation: on touch line
  • Keep dribbling to coach if you have the space
  • Pass if you don't
  • Communication from teammates, yell "pressure" to release
  • Communication from teammates, yell "you have time" to dribble

Good Pass, Play O, Bad Pass Play D - Transitions

  • Goal throws ball to first player
  • Player attempts to pass through gate
  • If pass goes through gate, take off an become an option for leading pass
  • Try to pass before next gate to teammate that should be moving after the pass
  • If Player misses first gate, Second Player becomes offence
  • First player immediately had to transition from Offense to Defense
  • P2 is trying to score on keeper
  • P1 is trying to defend, not waiting on it
Why is this important?
  • P1 is transition to D to O if they make a good pass
  • P1 is transitioning quickly back to D if they make a bad pass
  • Promotes movement on O side if successful
  • There is a consequence for a bad pass
