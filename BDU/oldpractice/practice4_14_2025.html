Feint Cones

Pass, Move, Communicate

    Rules

    • Start with a Pass
    • Then a move, player without ball
    • Then repeat
    • Goal is to get the ball in the circles
    • Team is through when all players make it in the circle
    • Communication is big on who moves and where the pass is going
    • Start being turn based, then remove that restriction

Shoulder to Shoulder attack

    Rules

    • Match up
    • Person with ball is trying to dirbble forwad, but slow
    • Defender is trying to steal the ball
    • We are looking for body contact
    • we are looking to make sure we are not just running along side
    • Have defender come in at an agle, see how they adjust
