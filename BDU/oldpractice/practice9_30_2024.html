Diagonal 6 shooting

  • Pass on Diagonal
  • Opposite line 1 touch shoots
  • Rotate back and forth
  • After 5 or so minutes, add defender should to shoulder(second person in line)

1 Touch 6 shooting

  • Pass across the six
  • Focus on 1 touch scoring
  • rotate back and forth which line is the passer and shooter
  • After 5 or so minutes, add defender should to shoulder(second person in line)

Square pass, to space, shoot

  • Play square pass
  • Receiver plays diagonal
  • Diagonal can be played left and right of defender (defender is sticks)
  • Get used to playing off left or right or straight up
  • Diagonal passer should sprint to where they passed
  • Add a real defender to see how it plays out

Gauntlet

  • Three boxes
  • 1 Goalie, 1 defender in each of the last two, one free space
  • 2 or 3 players work the ball down
  • defenders cannot leave their grid
  • Make it past the last grid, shoot on goalie
  • Ext of 3 on 3 work
Do this with each team a few times before we start the scrimage

Kick Off Option - Lets call it #2

  • Forward does direct pass back to Defender (rule change in 2016)
  • Outside mid horizontal run inside
  • Inside create outside options
  • Defender keep head up, dribble or release to option
