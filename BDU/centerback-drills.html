Center Back Training

This tactical training session focuses on essential game elements: Headers for both attacking and defensive situations; precise Passing techniques to maintain possession; executing Switch to the Left plays to change the point of attack; defending Gap Balls through proper positioning and anticipation; developing Positive First Touch to maintain control under pressure; and mastering Driven Passes to break defensive lines. Each element is crucial for building a well-rounded tactical understanding of the game.
