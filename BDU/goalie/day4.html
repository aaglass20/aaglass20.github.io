Day 4: Crosses & 1v1 Situations

Commanding the box and winning duels

Drill 1: Catching Crosses

15 minutes With Server Intermediate

Crosses from wide positions. Focus on timing the jump, catching at highest point, and protecting yourself while in the air.

  • Call "KEEPER!" loudly and early
  • Attack the ball - don't wait for it
  • Take off on one foot, drive knee up for protection
  • Catch at highest point with two hands
  • Bring ball into chest immediately

Drill 2: Punching

15 minutes With Server Intermediate

When you can't catch, punch it clear. Two-fist punch for height and distance. Clear the danger area, don't punch it back into traffic.

  • Two fists together, thumbs on top
  • Punch through the bottom half of ball
  • Aim for height and distance
  • Punch toward the sidelines, not centrally
  • Call "AWAY!" so teammates know to clear

Drill 3: 1v1 Closing Down

15 minutes With Attacker Advanced

Attacker breaks through, keeper must decide when to come out. Make yourself big, stay on feet as long as possible.

  • Come out quickly while ball is traveling
  • Slow down as you get close - be balanced
  • Stay on feet - don't go to ground early
  • Make yourself big - arms out, body spread
  • Force attacker wide or into a bad touch

Drill 4: Breakaway Saves

20 minutes Game Situation Advanced

Full breakaway situations. Through ball played, keeper and attacker race. Keeper must decide: come out and smother, or stay and make save?

  • Read the pass - can you get there first?
  • If yes: sprint out, get ball before attacker
  • If no: set position, make yourself big
  • Spread technique: lead with hands, body follows
  • Be brave but not reckless

Key Coaching Points

  • Communication: LOUD and EARLY calls for crosses
  • Bravery: Attack the ball, don't be afraid of contact
  • Decision Making: Come or stay? Make decision early and commit
  • Stay Big: On 1v1s, stay on feet and spread wide
  • Recovery: If beaten, get up fast - play isn't over
