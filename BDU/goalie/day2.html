Day 2: Positioning & Angles

Cutting down angles and being in the right place

Drill 1: Arc Movement

10 minutes Individual Beginner

Practice moving along the arc. As ball moves, keeper shuffles to stay on the line between ball and center of goal.

  • Imagine a string from ball to center of goal
  • Stay on that line as ball moves
  • Shuffle, don't cross feet
  • Stay in set position while moving

Drill 2: Cutting Angles

15 minutes With Shooter Intermediate

Shooter at different positions, keeper adjusts position. Coach freezes play to check if keeper is in optimal position before shot is taken.

  • Come off line to cut angle - but not too far
  • Further from goal = come out more
  • Close to goal = stay closer to line
  • Always be set before the shot

Drill 3: Near Post Positioning

15 minutes With Shooters Intermediate

Shots from wide angles. Focus on protecting near post while still covering far post. Near post is YOUR responsibility.

  • Never get beaten at near post
  • Position to see ball and cover near post
  • Slight lean toward near post
  • Far post = dive and stretch territory

Drill 4: Movement and Set

20 minutes With Passers Advanced

Ball passed between players, keeper adjusts position with each pass. Random player shoots - keeper must be set and in position.

  • Adjust position with every pass
  • Quick feet, stay balanced
  • Be set before the shot - no lunging
  • Read the play - anticipate where shot comes from

Key Coaching Points

  • The Arc: Move along the arc, not in straight lines
  • Near Post: Never get beaten at your near post
  • Set Position: Always be balanced and set before shot
  • Come Out: Cut the angle, make goal look smaller
  • Don't Over-commit: Come out but stay on your feet
