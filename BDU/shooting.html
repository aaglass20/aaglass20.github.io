Shooter Vs Defense

  • 5 balls lined up at 18 yard line
  • Striker on ones side, Defender on other
  • Defense is only allowed to shuffle and get body in front to stop strike
  • Sticker runs back and forth and only shoots when finding and opening
  • D is only trying to block
Why is this important?
  • Works on body placement for D
  • Urgency on closing in for D
  • Works on quick shot
