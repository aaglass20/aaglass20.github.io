Group 1: Dribbling

Dribbling

  • Red Cones, Right Foot
  • Blue Cones, Right Foot
  • Yellow cones, both feet
  • Time run to cone
Group 2: Shooting + Goal Keeping

Dribbling

  • First in line starts at goalie
  • Second in line shoots
  • Shoot to score, Goalie defends
  • After shot, shooter become goalie
  • Goalie to the end of the line
  • Repeat
Group 3: Long Pass

Long Pass

  • Long pass, try to score points (vid shows 5, just do 1)
  • Go to the back of the line
  • Repeat
  • 2 points middle, 1 point side, 0 Miss, 0 Short
Group 4: Receive to short pass

Receive to short pass

  • Coach passes to player
  • They control it inside the square, pass it back
  • Do 1 try, back to line, next up
  • Pass back, 2 points for middle, 1 point for side, 0 miss
Group 5: Defend the Goal

Defend the Goal

  • Coach passes to player in random spots
  • Goal is to clear to the sides, between the target
  • 5 balls then rotate
