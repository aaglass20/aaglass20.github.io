Group 1: Dribbling - Sharks and Minos

Dribbling

  • One player starts as Shark
  • Minnow dribble and avoid Shark
  • Shark clears ball of Minnow
  • lat one cleared becomes the Shark for the next game
Group 2: Soccer Bowling

Soccer Bowling

  • Line up as many tall cones you can in a goal
  • Player gets 3 shots to knock over as many as possible
  • Make it a competition to see how may total you can knockdown after 2-3 rounds
  • We should try to keep the focus on proper passing over shooting to work on accuracy
Group 3: Finishing Drill

1 Timers

  • Coach passes ball to moving player
  • Player 1 touch shot
  • Have enough balls to go through the drill for everyone
  • Reload balls, repeat
  • Switch sides to use both feet
Why is this important?
  • Focus on quick strike
  • Focus on placement
