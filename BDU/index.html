BDU U10 Practice

Group 1 - Through Ball Drill

  • Work right or left side
  • Runner explodes after pass back
  • receiver of pass back can yell drop>
  • When playing through, say Name and through

Group 2 - Passing Ladder.

  • Right foot Up
  • Left foot back
  • On toes, light feet
  • Move to ball, don't wait
  • Can be 1 touch
  • Can be 2 touch
  • Coach calls out if 1 or 2 touch
  • Urgent and focus

Group 3 - Trap Control

  • Maintain control on each line
  • Pass back after controlled
  • line 1, low control
  • line 2, mid high control
  • line 2, chest control
  • Right foot up the ladder
  • Left foot down the ladder
  • Shuffle body
  • don't raise foot, keep it low

Whole Team - Work Sides, Play Through.

  • D1 pass to outside
  • D2 Turn to outside
  • D2 takes ball forward
  • Coach then pressures D2 to force pass
  • D2 passes up line or plays through
  • Nears M or F communicates they have it
  • The other separates
  • Work ball down field for shot
  • Could add Goalie
  • Could add Defender
