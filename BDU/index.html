Group 3 - Defend the Goal

  • 5-6 Balls
  • Player is making sure coach does not score
  • Work on shuffling body to ball opposed to raising foot
  • Defending and keeping arms down
  • Work Left, right, Up, down
  • Be urgent, get rid of ball quick
  • Play to teammate
  • Not shown in vid, but teammate could work turning the ball out
Why is this important?
  • Stresses urgency of getting to ball in danger zone
  • Works moving body opposed to limbs
  • Works trapping on all levels, low, high, medium
  • Keeps mind set of going outside, playing to a teammte or zone

Whole Team - Work Sides, Play Through.

  • D1 pass to outside
  • D2 Turn to outside
  • D2 takes ball forward
  • Coach then pressures D2 to force pass
  • D2 passes up line or plays through
  • Nears M or F communicates they have it
  • The other separates
  • Work ball down field for shot
  • Could add Goalie
  • Could add Defender
Why is this important?
  • Works on importance of playing outside
  • Works on turning out, instead of turning in
  • Works on carrying ball when not pressure
  • Works on keeping head up to look for pressure
  • Works on releasing ball with pressure
  • Can have teammates identify pressure to help teammates
  • Works through ball and communicating the intended receiver
  • Works simple pass and finish down the filed
