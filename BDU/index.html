  • 4 Players on outside with ball
  • 2 in the middle
  • 2 minute timer
  • Player on outside Throws into the box

Good Pass, Play O, Bad Pass Play D - Transitions

  • Goal throws ball to first player
  • Player attempts to pass through gate
  • If pass goes through gate, take off an become an option for leading pass
  • Try to pass before next gate to teammate that should be moving after the pass
  • If Player misses first gate, Second Player becomes offence
  • First player immediately had to transition from Offense to Defense
  • P2 is trying to score on keeper
  • P1 is trying to defend, not waiting on it
Why is this important?
  • P1 is transition to D to O if they make a good pass
  • P1 is transitioning quickly back to D if they make a bad pass
  • Promotes movement on O side if successful
  • There is a consequence for a bad pass
