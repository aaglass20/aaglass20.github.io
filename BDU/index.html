  • 2nd grid, 1 touch
  • 3rd Grid 2 touch right
  • 2nd grid, 1 touch
  • 1st grid, 2 touch left
  • Keep head up

  • move quick

  • Use of both feet
  • Processing dif skills in different grids
  • Horizontal movement

Group 2 - 1 timer drill - Matt

  • Line up on on side of goal
  • Player starts to run accorss
  • Coach plays pass
  • Player strikes 1 timer
  • Do from both sides
  • Move Quickly, hustle back
  • Use Right, Left, inside, and outside foot
Why is this important?
  • Works quick strike
  • Works on quick power and reaction
  • This Drill also is good for defense, think of it as a quick clear
  • Works on quick power and reaction
  • Works placement

Group 3 - Defend the Goal - Chris

  • 5-6 Balls
  • Player is making sure coach does not score
  • Work on shuffling body to ball opposed to raising foot
  • Defending and keeping arms down
  • Work Left, right, Up, down
  • Be urgent, get rid of ball quick
  • Play to teammate
  • Not shown in vid, but teammate could work turning the ball out
Why is this important?
  • Stresses urgency of getting to ball in danger zone
  • Works moving body opposed to limbs
  • Works trapping on all levels, low, high, medium
  • Keeps mind set of going outside, playing to a teammte or zone

Defensive Technique as a team

  • Offence slow dribbles
  • Warm up with "Flip" drill
  • Defense channels with "running like a Defender"
  • Shift hips if Off changes direction
  • Coach yells attack, attack
  • Lunge across Offences body
  • Arm first, post on body
  • Step across body and tackle
  • We look for openings to steal
  • Try it without coach influence
Why is this important?
  • Stepping across body slows down offence if you miss your tackle attempt
  • We don't poke, we body up
  • Be confident

Whole Team - Hold the line

  • Line 3 players up at midfield
  • Line up 3 Defenders
  • Coach playes ball between the lanes to create situations
  • Another Coach behind goalies
  • Scneario 1: play ball through defense, goalie reaction, boot ball to side or out of bounds
  • Scenario 2: play ball to either side, if midfield gets beyond defence, switch off help
  • Coach can help players to indentify backside help
  • backside help should go directly to ball and stat defending closely, attempting to retrieve
Why is this important?
  • Goalie reacting to bail out defense, goalie should call off D
  • Work on help and communication on defense
  • Defense should get legally handsy

Fun drill if we have time, make it a competition

Shooter Vs Defence

  • 5 balls lined up at 18 yard line
  • Striker on ones side, Defender on other
  • Defense is only allowed to shuffle and get body in front to stop strike
  • Sticker runs back and forth and only shoots when finding and opening
  • D is only trying to block
Why is this important?
  • Works on body placement for D
  • Urgency on closing in for D
  • Works on quick shot
