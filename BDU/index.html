Shoulder to Shoulder Review

    Rules

    • Match up
    • Lets start with Person with ball is trying to dirbble forwad, but slow
    • Defender is trying to steal the ball
    • We are looking for body contact
    • we are looking to make sure we are not just running along side
    • Have defender come in at an angle, see how they adjust
    • Once we see progress, go live. Player with ball is trying to score
    • Try to shoot from Goal Box
    • Def should work on putting there arem across to slow down opponent
    • This establishes upper hand

Simple Give & Go

    Rules

    • Play with ball starts with dribble
    • Passes Left or Right
    • Player accelerates and motions where they want ball played
    • Receiver plays ball to spot
    • Runner takes one setup touch and shoots
    • Runner goes to line they played
    • Player that received the ball goes to the runner line

Throw In Control

    Rules

    • 3 Cones
    • Thrower at 1, Receiver at 1, 1 to Check to
    • Receiver checks to cone, comes back to other
    • Thrower hits feet with throw
    • Variations:
    • Play back to Thrower
    • Outside Touch (of foot)
    • Inside Touch (of foot)
    • Feint Turn
    • Start focus on first 3 variations
