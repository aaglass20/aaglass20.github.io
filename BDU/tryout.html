Weave to Shoot

    This trial is to work on speed, control, and the transition to a shot

    • Each player gets 3 attempts
    • Time run until the final cone is reached
    • Missing a cone is a 1 second penalty per cone
    • Shot must be taken before the 18
    • 2 point for sides, 1 point middle, 0 miss or over the line

Roll-On, Roll-Off 3v3

    Teams of three compete. When the attacking team loses possession, they are immediately out. Their opponents become attackers. The next waiting team enters the field as the new defenders. This rotation continues throughout the drill.

    Setup:

    • Play on a smaller field or 20 x 10-yard field with mini-goals or dimensions you prefer
    • Divide players into teams of three (no goalkeepers).
    • Two teams start on the field while the remaining teams wait and observe from the sidelines.

    Instructions:

    • Two teams play at a time: one attacking, one defending.
    • If attackers score, defenders are eliminated; a new defending team enters
    • If attackers score, they remain on the field and restart with possession.
    • If defenders gain possession, attackers are eliminated, and defenders switch to attacking.
    • A new defending team comes on field
    • Players on the sidelines must stay alert for their turn.
