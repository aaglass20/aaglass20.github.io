Small-Sided Games

Capture the Flag warmup with bals

6 balls in center, 4 bases, 4 players. Each player tries to capture as many balls as they can. Players are allowed to steal balls.

Hungry Hippos Relay

A fast-paced, competitive soccer drill that mimics the excitement of Hungry Hippos while developing essential soccer skills.

Sharks and Minnows

Fun game where one player (the shark) tries to tag the other players (the minnows) as they dribble across or around a designated area.

Sharks and Minnows with Passing

3 players start as sharks, 4 players as minnows. Minnows dribble and pass to avoid sharks. If a Minnow looses their ball, they become a passing option for other minnows.
