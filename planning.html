testing skills

test
planning

Strategy first. Coverage maps, risk-based approaches, and test plans that people actually read and reference. Good planning saves more bugs than any single tool.

tools & approaches

the approach

testrail
Test case management, run tracking, and milestone reporting. Used to manage thousands of test cases across multiple products and teams.
view notes →
mindmup
Visual coverage mapping. Start with a feature, branch out to all the ways it could break. The map becomes the test plan.
view notes →
⚖️
risk-based testing
Prioritizing what to test based on probability of failure and business impact. Not everything deserves the same coverage depth.
🔬
exploratory testing
Structured exploration without scripts. Session-based testing with charters — finding the bugs that test cases never would.
📋
test case design
Writing cases that are maintainable, not just thorough. Equivalence partitioning, boundary analysis, and decision tables in practice.
🚀
release readiness
Go/no-go criteria, defect triage, and communicating risk to stakeholders clearly. Quality is a business decision, not just a pass/fail.
