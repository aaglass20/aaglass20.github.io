"Fail Fast, Learn Faster"

This is a game of failure. We teach resilience through errors and strikeouts. Great players fail fast and learn faster.

You will make mistakes. The key is how you respond. Have a short memory - use each mistake as a stepping stone to get better.
Find your calm. Stay composed under pressure and trust your preparation.

Quick Practice Tips

  • Always warm up properly before practice - 5-10 minutes of light jogging and dynamic stretching
  • Focus on quality repetitions over quantity - proper form builds muscle memory
  • Watch videos at half speed to catch important technique details
  • Practice with a purpose - set specific goals for each training session
