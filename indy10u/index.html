Quick Practice Tips

  • Always warm up properly before practice - 5-10 minutes of light jogging and dynamic stretching
  • Focus on quality repetitions over quantity - proper form builds muscle memory
  • Watch videos at half speed to catch important technique details
  • Practice with a purpose - set specific goals for each training session
