Rules & Payouts

NCAA Men's Basketball Tournament Squares Pool

There is a 100-square grid — each square will be sold for $25, making a total pot of $2,000.00 for the main board.

After all the squares have been sold, the numbers will be randomly assigned. The additional $500.00 will be used for the *inverse numbers board.

The same numbers will be used for the FINAL 63 GAMES in the tournament for the main board. The inverse of your numbers will be used for the FINAL 15 GAMES in the tournament for the *inverse board.

  • For the main board: the Round of 64 thru the National Championship. The 4 First Round games will not be used.
  • For the inverse board: the Regional Semifinals thru the National Championship. The 4 First Round games and the Round of 64 and Round of 32 will not be used.

The last digits of the final score of each game will be used to determine the winning squares.

The numbers across the top of the grid will represent the WINNING teams. The numbers down the left side of the grid will represent the LOSING teams.
