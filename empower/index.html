A Message for Coaches

Hello Coaches,

I'm excited to share that Blue Devil United is partnering with Empower Sports and the iCan Soccer program this fall!

Empower Sports provides adapted athletic, fitness, recreational, and social opportunities for individuals with physical, sensory, or developmental disabilities. Through this partnership, we're hoping to make a meaningful impact in our community while giving our players an opportunity to serve others through the game they love.

The program will run each Saturday from August 22 through September 26, with no session on September 19, from 10:00–11:15 AM on the fields in front of the Independence Field House Fields.

We're asking each BDU coach to have their team volunteer for one Saturday during the program. Ideally, we'd love to have two teams represented each week. Players, coaches, and parents are all encouraged to participate.

What will the volunteers be doing? It's simple:

  • Help players work on basic soccer skills and encourage them during the first 45 minutes.
  • Join the participants during the final 30 minutes of small-sided games by cheering them on, assisting them, or even playing alongside them.

This is much more than a volunteer opportunity. It's a chance for our players to build friendships, develop leadership, and experience the joy that comes from making a positive difference in someone else's life. Those who have volunteered in the past know that you often leave feeling like you received more than you gave.

Please talk with your team and sign up for the Saturday that works best for you. We'll work to fill each week's spots with two teams.

Thank you for helping Blue Devil United continue to make a positive impact both on and off the field. We look forward to seeing our BDU family come together to support this incredible program!

Go BDU! 💙💛

How It Works

Simple, impactful, and an incredible experience for everyone involved

👥

Two Teams Per Week

We're asking each coach to bring their team for one Saturday. Ideally, two BDU teams will join each session.

⚽

Skills Block (45 min)

BDU players help iCan participants work on basic soccer skills — passing, dribbling, and shooting with smiles!

🏆

Small-Sided Games (30 min)

Cheer, assist, or play alongside participants in fun small-sided games. Energy and encouragement are your biggest tools!

❤️

Give More Than You Take

Past volunteers always leave feeling they received more than they gave. This is a life-changing experience for your players.
