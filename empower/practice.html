Volunteer Quick-Guide

Keep these tips in mind every week — they make all the difference

😊

Patience First

Celebrate every small win loudly. A smile and "great job!" means the world.

🤲

Follow Their Lead

Let participants set the pace. Your job is to encourage, not to coach perfectly.

🗣️

Simple Language

Use short, clear instructions. Demonstrate more than you explain.

🎉

Make It Fun

Energy is contagious. Bring enthusiasm and creativity to every activity.

Week 1 · August 22, 2026

Ball Familiarization & Passing

First session — focus on FUN and making participants feel welcome!

⚽
10:00
🌟 Welcome & Warm-Up 10 min
  • 👋 Greet every participant by name — high-fives all around!
  • ⚽ Ball Roll Tag — everyone rolls the ball with their foot around a circle. No competition, just touch! Fun
  • 🙆 Seated or standing stretches with the ball (toe taps, ankle rolls, arm circles)
  • 💡 BDU players pair up 1:1 with each participant right away
💡 Set the tone: energy, smiles, and patience. Many participants may be meeting BDU players for the first time.
10:10
🦶 Ball Touch Station 15 min
  • ⚽ Sole Taps — tap the top of the ball alternating feet while stationary Skill
  • ↔️ Side-to-Side Rolls — roll ball left and right with sole of foot
  • 🔵 Toe-Ball-Toe — push ball forward with toe, pull back with sole, repeat in place
  • 🌟 BDU players demo first, then guide their partner through each move
💡 Celebrate any ball contact as a win. For wheelchair participants, use hands or a ramp assist — any interaction counts!
10:25
🎯 Partner Passing 20 min
  • 🟡 Close Passes — 3–4 feet apart, inside-of-foot passes. Focus on stopping the ball. Skill
  • 📏 Step Back — after 3 successful passes, each pair takes one step back. How far can you go?
  • 🎵 Passing to Music — pass on the beat when music plays, freeze when it stops Fun
  • 💬 Encourage cheering between pairs — "Great pass!", "You've got this!"
💡 Adjust distance based on each participant's ability. Closer is always fine — the goal is connection, not distance.
10:45
🏆 Small-Sided Games 30 min
  • 🔵 3v3 "Everybody Scores" — small goals, no goalkeeper, each team needs every player to score before they can win Team
  • 🎉 BDU players celebrate every goal (for either team!) with maximum energy
  • 🤝 Rotate BDU players onto both teams to keep numbers even and energy high
  • ⏱️ Short 5-min games with water breaks — keep it moving and upbeat
💡 Nobody keeps score in a way that "loses." Celebrate all goals, all efforts. End with a big group celebration!
Week 2 · August 29, 2026

Dribbling & Ball Control

Move with the ball — creative, fun, and confidence-building!

🌀
10:00
🌟 Welcome Back & Warm-Up 10 min
  • 👋 Reunite with last week's partner — familiar faces matter!
  • ⚽ Dribble Parade — everyone dribbles freely in a big area, BDU players make it silly Fun
  • 🙆 Leg swings and hip circles to loosen up
💡 Returning participants will be more comfortable. Build on those connections from Week 1!
10:10
🌀 Cone Dribbling Course 15 min
  • 🔶 Set up a simple weave of 5 cones per pair — BDU player dribbles first to demo Skill
  • ⚽ Participants dribble through at their own pace — BDU player walks alongside
  • 🌈 Crazy Cones — add silly challenges (dribble while humming, stop on clap, change direction on whistle) Fun
  • 🏅 Cheer loudly when they reach the end!
10:25
🚦 Red Light / Green Light 20 min
  • 🟢 Green Light — dribble forward! Skill
  • 🔴 Red Light — stop the ball with your sole (no hands)
  • 🟡 Yellow Light — dribble in tiny circles
  • 💙 Blue Light (bonus BDU round) — dribble backwards!
  • 🎉 BDU players play too — make it a shared experience Team
💡 Call colors loudly and with energy. The stopping skill is key — give extra praise for clean stops.
10:45
🏆 Dribble King/Queen Games 30 min
  • 👑 Square Wars — everyone dribbles in a square, try to kick other balls out while keeping yours in Fun
  • ⚽ 3v3 with Dribble Bonus — a goal counts double if scored after dribbling past a defender
  • 🤝 BDU players guide and protect their partners during possession Team
💡 Keep the square wars low-pressure — join in yourself and let them knock YOUR ball out for cheers!
Week 3 · September 5, 2026

Shooting & Finishing

Every player loves scoring a goal — make this one memorable!

🎯
10:00
🌟 Welcome & Warm-Up 10 min
  • 👋 High-energy greetings — BDU players are hype squad from the moment participants arrive!
  • ⚽ Goal Parade — each player dribbles to the goal and shoots freely to warm up Fun
  • 🙆 Kick-swings and ankle circles for shooting preparation
10:10
🎯 Shooting Technique 15 min
  • 🦶 Stationary Kicks — ball on the ground, practice laces-kick with a BDU player holding the ball still Skill
  • 📏 Start 5 ft from goal, move back one step for every goal scored
  • 🌟 BDU players celebrate EVERY shot, even misses — "So close! Try again!"
  • 🎯 Target Shooting — use cones or discs to mark corners of the goal as targets Skill
💡 For participants who can't kick, try hand-throw into the goal or a ramp. A goal is a goal — celebrate it all!
10:25
⚽ "Score a Goal" Challenge 20 min
  • 🏃 Simple dribble → shoot sequence — BDU player sets the ball up, participant finishes Skill
  • 🔊 Group celebrates every goal with a chant: "1… 2… 3… BDU!" Team
  • 🎉 Goal Counter — keep a running team total on a whiteboard or cones. Can we hit 50 goals today?! Fun
💡 Keep the line moving — short bursts, quick celebrations, next player up. Energy stays high when everyone is active.
10:45
🏆 Shootout Tournament 30 min
  • 🥊 Bracket-style penalty shootout — 3 shots each, BDU player and participant shoot together as a team Team
  • 🏆 Everyone gets a trophy round — final round is all-makes-count. No one leaves without scoring!
  • ⚽ 3v3 games alongside shootout for participants who want more time on the ball
💡 The shootout is the highlight — load it with drama and hype even for the simplest shots. "And the crowd goes WILD!"
Week 4 · September 12, 2026

Teamwork & Playing Together

Building bonds through the game — the best part of soccer!

🤝
10:00
🌟 Team Intro & Warm-Up 10 min
  • 👥 Form teams of 4 (2 BDU + 2 participants) — name your team! Fun
  • ⚽ Juggle Relay — team sees how many total touches they can get (thigh, foot, head) without the ball dropping
  • 💪 Team cheer/chant — give each team 2 minutes to invent one
10:10
🤝 Passing Combinations 15 min
  • 🔁 Triangle Passing — 3-person triangles, pass and move to the open cone Skill
  • 📢 Call name before passing — "Sarah!" then kick
  • 💛 1-2 Combo — pass to BDU player, get it back (wall pass), then shoot. Simple but empowering! Skill
💡 Calling names builds connection. Participants will light up when their name is called before a pass!
10:25
🎮 Team Challenges 20 min
  • 🏁 Relay Race — dribble to cone and back, tag next teammate. Silly rules: dribble with weak foot only, hop, etc. Fun
  • 🎯 Team Target Practice — team combines passes before shooting, each touch adds a multiplier to the goal Team
  • 📸 Encourage BDU players to cheer for other teams too — model good sportsmanship
10:45
🏆 Team Tournament 30 min
  • 🏟️ 4v4 small-sided with teams from warm-up — BDU players play ON the participant teams Team
  • 🌟 Fair-Play Points — extra points for encouraging opponents, helping someone up, and team celebrations
  • 🎉 End with all teams in a circle — each player shares one word about their experience: "fun", "happy", "awesome"! Fun
💡 Fair-play points matter more than the score. Reward sportsmanship loudly so everyone sees it.
Week 5 · September 26, 2026 · FINAL SESSION

All-Star Celebration!

The grand finale — showcase everything they've learned and celebrate together!

🏆
🎉
Final Session! This is the culmination of the whole program. Go all-out on celebration, energy, and making each participant feel like a superstar. Consider bringing extra snacks or small tokens of appreciation!
10:00
🌟 Red Carpet Welcome 10 min
  • 🎺 BDU players form a tunnel/line and cheer every participant in by name as they arrive
  • 🏅 Each participant gets a "All-Star" ribbon or sticker when they arrive Fun
  • ⚽ Free-touch warm-up — just play freely and soak in the atmosphere
💡 Start the energy at 11 from minute one. This is THEIR day!
10:10
🎪 Skills Showcase Stations 20 min
  • 🦶 Station 1 — Touch Challenge: how many sole taps in 30 seconds? Beat your personal best from Week 1! Skill
  • 🌀 Station 2 — Dribble Course: timed cone weave — everyone cheers! Skill
  • 🎯 Station 3 — Long Shot: shoot from the farthest distance they're comfortable with Skill
  • 📸 BDU players act as commentators for each station Fun
10:30
⚽ "Best of" Favorites 15 min
  • 🗳️ Let participants vote on their favorite game from the program
  • 🎮 Play the top voted activity one last time — full energy! Fun
  • 💛 BDU players share what they'll remember most from the season
10:45
🏆 All-Star Championship Game 20 min
  • 🏟️ Big mixed game — ALL participants and ALL BDU players together on one field Team
  • 🌟 No sides — it's a "pass-around" where everyone tries to score on any goal, celebrate every touch
  • 🎉 Count goals out loud as a group — "FIFTY! FIFTY-ONE!" Fun
11:05
🎗️ Closing Ceremony 10 min
  • 🏅 Recognition for every participant — say each name aloud and have BDU players clap Team
  • 💛 Group photo opportunity — everyone together!
  • 🤝 BDU coaches/players share closing words about what the experience meant to them
  • 💙 Final cheer: "1… 2… 3… iCan!" → "1… 2… 3… BDU!"
💡 End on the highest possible note. Send participants home feeling celebrated, seen, and loved by the BDU family.
